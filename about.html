About the Band

Sorry-Man is a high-energy fusion of rock, funk, and a dash of chaos based out of Bozeman, Montana. Formed in 2024, the band has been shaking up local venues with electrifying performances and unforgettable grooves. Whether they're headlining festivals or jamming in underground clubs, Sorry-Man delivers music that makes you move, laugh, and sometimes even think.
